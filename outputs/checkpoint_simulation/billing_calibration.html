Interpretation Guide

  • MAE: Average absolute difference between simulated and billed therms. Lower is better.
  • RMSE: Root mean square error. Penalizes larger errors more heavily than MAE.
  • Mean Bias: Average signed difference. Positive = model overestimates, negative = underestimates.
  • R²: Proportion of variance in billed therms explained by simulated therms (0-1 scale). Higher is better.
  • Correlation: Pearson correlation coefficient between simulated and billed therms (-1 to 1).
  • Scatter Plot: Red dashed line represents perfect agreement (1:1). Points above the line indicate overestimation; below indicate underestimation.
