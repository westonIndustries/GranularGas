## Scenario Assumptions

### Baseline Scenario Parameters

| Parameter | Value | Description |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| **Electrification Rate** | 2% annual | Gradual fuel switching from gas to electric/heat pump |
| **Efficiency Improvement** | 1% annual | Equipment efficiency gains from replacements |
| **Housing Growth** | 0% | No new construction (static housing stock) |
| **Equipment Replacement** | Weibull model | Age-based replacement with probabilistic timing |
| **Weather Normalization** | 30-year normals | Uses NOAA climate normals (1991-2020) |

### Key Drivers of Demand Decline

1. **Equipment Replacement:** Aging equipment replaced with more efficient models
2. **Electrification:** 2% annual switching from gas to electric/heat pump (primarily space heating)
3. **Efficiency Improvements:** New equipment has higher efficiency ratings
4. **Weather Normalization:** Consistent weather assumptions across all years

## Data Quality and Coverage

### Data Sources Used

- **NW Natural Premise Data:** 650,000 active residential customers
- **Equipment Inventory:** ~1.3M equipment units across all end-uses
- **Weather Data:** 11 weather stations covering service territory
- **RBSA Building Stock:** 2022 NEEA data for building characteristics
- **Tariff Data:** Historical rates (2005-2025) for billing calibration

### Data Gaps and Mitigations

| Gap | Impact | Mitigation |
|-----|--------|-----------|
| Missing equipment efficiency | MEDIUM | Filled with national DOE/RECS defaults |
| Incomplete billing data | LOW | Rows with null values excluded from calibration |
| RBSA coverage gaps | LOW | Used defaults for unmatched premises |
| Weather station assignments | LOW | Assigned to nearest station by district |

## Model Limitations

### Spatial Resolution
- Results aggregated to district level, not premise-level
- Weather station assignments are static
- Geographic drill-down limited to county/district

### Temporal Resolution
- Annual aggregation only (no monthly/daily outputs)
- Billing data aggregated to annual
- Weather normalization uses 30-year averages

### Calibration Scope
- Model calibrated to NW Natural service territory only
- Equipment efficiency based on national averages
- Weibull parameters from DOE/NEMS, not NW Natural specific

### Model Assumptions
- Weibull shape parameters (beta) are fixed
- Baseload factors assume constant annual consumption
- Linear HDD/CDD relationship for weather normalization
