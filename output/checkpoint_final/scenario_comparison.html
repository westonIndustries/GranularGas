Key Findings

  • The baseline scenario projects a gradual UPC decline of 11.3% over the 10-year period (2025-2035)
  • The high electrification scenario shows a steeper UPC decline of 22.3%, driven by accelerated fuel switching
  • By 2035, the high electrification scenario reduces total demand by 46.7M therms (12.5%) compared to baseline
  • Space heating represents the largest end-use category in both scenarios, accounting for ~35% of total demand
  • Electrification primarily impacts space heating and water heating, with minimal effect on cooking and drying
